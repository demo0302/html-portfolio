My top 3 movie of all-time.

Parasite(2019)

A captivating and suspenseful film that masterfully combines elements of horror, thriller, and psychological thriller genres. It tells the story of a poor family infiltrating a wealthy family's household, leading to a series of chilling and thought-provoking events.

The Silence of the Lambs(1991)

A classic psychological thriller featuring a young FBI cadet seeking the help of a former psychiatrist and incarcerated cannibal murderer to locate a serial killer. The film is a masterclass in suspense and character development.

Get Out(2017)

A unique horror-thriller with a psychological twist, this film follows a young Black man visiting his white girlfriend's family in the countryside, where he finds himself in increasingly creepy situations. The movie is a powerful exploration of race and identity.
